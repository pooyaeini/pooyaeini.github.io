01 — Profile

About

I am a physician-researcher with a focused profile in cardiovascular medicine, cardiac imaging, and clinical artificial intelligence. My work centers on machine-learning–based diagnostic and prognostic models — particularly in echocardiography, ultrasound-based cardiovascular assessment, multimodal clinical prediction, and risk stratification.

I have extensive experience in systematic reviews, meta-analyses, study design, scientific writing, and the evaluation of AI models for clinically meaningful cardiovascular outcomes. I care about models that are not only accurate, but well-calibrated, validated, and genuinely useful at the bedside.

Currently Research Assistant, Shaheed Rajaie Cardiovascular Medical & Research Center

02 — Focus Areas

Research Focus

Cardiac Imaging AI

Artificial intelligence across echocardiography, ultrasound, cardiac CT, and CMR — translating imaging signals into reliable clinical insight.

Risk Prediction Modeling

Prognostic models for heart failure, stroke, cardiometabolic and valvular disease, and post-procedural outcomes.

ML Model Evaluation

Performance synthesis, calibration, external validation, and clinical-utility assessment of diagnostic and prognostic algorithms.

Evidence Synthesis

Meta-analysis of AI-based diagnostic and prognostic models to establish what actually works in cardiovascular medicine.
